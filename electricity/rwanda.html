Access in electricy¶

The questions related to the access to electricity are in section C.

Rwanda’s greatest challenge is to provide access to at least basic electricity supply (Tier 1 or above) to households without any access (Tier 0).

To improve access to electricity among grid-connected households, Rwanda needs to improve Reliability, Quality, and Availability.
